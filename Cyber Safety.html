Cyber Safety

This page has information about how to stay safe online and I have also included a video you might like.

Stay safe on the internet

1. Don’t share your passwords

2. Don’t share your full name online

3. Tell an adult if you are being bullied online

4. Never download anything without a parent’s permission

5. Never click on ads

6. Tell an adult if you got an email from someone you don’t know

7. Don’t click on a link unless you’re sure it’s safe

8. Never post pictures or videos of yourself online

9. Never meet people who you met online

10. Think twice before you type

11. If something comes up on the screen that scares you minimize the screen and tell an adult
